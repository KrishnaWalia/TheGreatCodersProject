Sample Calisthenics Foundation Plan

This plan is designed to build foundational bodyweight strength, control, and endurance. The key is perfect form. Aura's AI Form Coach will be invaluable here.

Sample Daily Meal Plan (Lean & Clean)

  • Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with spinach and a side of avocado.
  • Lunch: Large bowl of quinoa with black beans, corn, bell peppers, and a lean protein source like shredded chicken or tofu.
  • Snack: Apple slices with natural peanut butter.
  • Dinner: Lean ground turkey stir-fry with a variety of colorful vegetables (broccoli, carrots, snap peas) and a light soy-ginger sauce.
  • General: Focus on whole, unprocessed foods. Maintain high protein intake to support muscle repair and growth.

Sample Weekly Workout Schedule (3-Day Full Body)

Focus on mastering the form of each exercise before increasing reps or moving to a harder variation. Aim for 3-4 sets per exercise.

  • Monday: Full Body Strength A
    • Push-ups (or incline/knee variations): 5-12 reps
    • Bodyweight Squats: 15-20 reps
    • Inverted Rows (using a table or bar): 5-12 reps
    • Plank: 30-60 seconds
    • Glute Bridges: 15-20 reps
  • Wednesday: Full Body Strength B
    • Dips (using chairs or parallel bars): 5-12 reps
    • Lunges: 10-15 reps per leg
    • Pull-up Negatives (or assisted pull-ups): 3-8 reps
    • Hanging Knee Raises: 10-15 reps
    • Pike Push-ups (for shoulders): 5-10 reps
  • Friday: Full Body Strength C
    • Repeat Day A, but try to increase reps or use a slightly harder variation. Focus on quality.
  • Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday, Sunday: Active Recovery & Skill Work - Light cardio, stretching, and practicing skills like handstands against a wall or working on pull-up form.
