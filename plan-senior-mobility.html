Sample Senior Mobility & Wellness Plan

This plan prioritizes joint health, balance, and cardiovascular fitness. All exercises should be performed carefully, focusing on control. The Aura AI can suggest modifications for any exercise.

Sample Daily Meal Plan (Nutrient-Dense)

  • Breakfast: 1 bowl of oatmeal with flax seeds and berries, 1 hard-boiled egg.
  • Lunch: A large salad with grilled chicken or chickpeas, lots of leafy greens, colorful vegetables, and a light vinaigrette.
  • Snack: A handful of almonds and an apple.
  • Dinner: 120g baked fish (like salmon), steamed asparagus, and a small portion of quinoa.
  • General: Ensure adequate calcium and Vitamin D intake through dairy or fortified foods. Stay well-hydrated throughout the day.

Sample Weekly Exercise Schedule

Consistency is more important than intensity. Listen to your body and rest when needed.

  • Monday: Light Cardio & Balance - 20-30 minute brisk walk. 10 minutes of balance exercises (e.g., standing on one leg near a support).
  • Tuesday: Seated Strength Training - Use light weights or resistance bands for exercises like seated rows, bicep curls, and leg extensions.
  • Wednesday: Flexibility & Mobility - Gentle stretching, chair yoga, or Tai Chi. Focus on all major joints.
  • Thursday: Light Cardio & Balance - Repeat Monday's routine or try water aerobics if available.
  • Friday: Functional Strength - Bodyweight exercises like chair squats (sitting down and standing up from a chair without using hands), wall push-ups, and step-ups on a low, stable step.
  • Saturday & Sunday: Active Rest - Light gardening, a leisurely walk, or gentle stretching.
